About Me

UX/UI designer & creative professional based in Toronto, Ontario

Sana Shepko is a UX/UI and graphic designer based in Toronto, Canada. She earned her BFA in Graphic Design from the State University of New York at New Paltz and her Master's from OCAD University. Sana is the manager and violinist of the Ukrainian folk band, Korinya, and performs with Korinya and other ensembles on a regular basis. In addition to UX/UI, Sana has experience in data visualization, print design, and motion design, and is strongly passionate about effective communication of concept, regardless of the medium.
