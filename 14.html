Teckle Farm

Website design and prototype

The Teckle Farm website and mobile app design is a project that I worked on for my internship at Shero Designs, a company that focuses on e-commerce website design, primarily through the implementation of Magento systems. I wireframed and prototyped the design layouts according to a grid, and then began the process of coding the final output. Linked in the images are the interactive Invision prototypes.
